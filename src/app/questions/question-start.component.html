Interview Wizard

Welcome! Please see the list of technical interview questions on the left. Selecting one will take you to a more detailed explanation. If you'd like to edit or add questions, please sign up or sign in. You are signed in and may edit questions.

This is a full-stack application, using Angular2 on the front-end (user interface), and Firebase on the back-end (data storage and authentication). Interview Wizard is under development by Van Boughner and you may find the source code on github.

Many thanks to those who worked on the underlying technologies, provided guidance, and stock photography: Angular, Angular CLI, Firebase, Udemy, and Pexels.
